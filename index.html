How to play

Select a ball.
Then click on a cell you'd like to move it.

Build a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line of the same color. The line of 5 or more balls disappears.

With each move, 3 new balls are spawned, — unless you've wiped a line.

Stay put as long as possible.
Good luck!
